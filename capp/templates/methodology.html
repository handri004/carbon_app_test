About Methodology:

At the heart of our methodology lies an extensive exploration, meticulously documented in a thoughtfully crafted paper.
Immersed in the realms of carbon emissions and green digitalization, this paper serves as the cornerstone of our initiatives.
Leveraging its insights, we proudly unveil our latest innovation: a state-of-the-art carbon emissions app. Engineered to empower
users, our app redefines how individuals interact with their environmental impact. Seamlessly integrating factors such as transportation
mode and distance, users can effortlessly assess their carbon footprint. Through this transformative tool, we aspire
to instill mindful decision-making and cultivate a sustainable future.

General Formulas

The General Formula:

\[\text{CO}_{2}=d*\frac{ef}{p}\]
In which: \[\text{CO}_2 \text{ = carbon emissions in grams} \] \[\text{d} \text{ = distance traveled in km} \] \[\text{ef} \text{ = emission factor in CO}_2 \text{/km} \] \[\text{d} \text{ = distance traveled in km} \]

The General Formula for Electric Vehicles:

\[ \text{ef = 31 * ec} \] In which: \[\text{ef} \text{ = emission factor in CO}_2 \text{/km} \] \[\text{31} \text{ = carbon intensity in gCO}_2 \text{/kwh} \] \[\text{ec} \text{ = energy consumption in kWh} \text{/km} \]
